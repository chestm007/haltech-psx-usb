## Notes

- The channel IDs from the binary (`_000`, `_001`, etc.) are internal ECU Manager identifiers, not the wire protocol selector IDs.
- The wire protocol uses 16-bit selector IDs (e.g., `0x0080`, `0x0294`) which are different from the internal `_NNN` identifiers.
- The `_NNN` identifiers are used for table/column/axis configuration in the 0x09 tune transfer protocol.
- The selector IDs (0x0080, 0x0294, etc.) are used for live data polling (0x0B protocol).
- The mapping between selector IDs and `_NNN` identifiers is not yet established — this requires live GUI cross-checking.
